I am a Machine Learning Engineer and Computer Engineering student (graduating in late 2026) from the Federal Technological University of Paraná (UTFPR), Brazil. My research focuses on efficient model adaptation and geospatial foundation models for environmental monitoring. I am an affiliated researcher at [CEIA-UFG](https://ceia.ufg.br/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}, one of the largest AI research centers in the world, where I bridge cutting-edge AI research and industry applications.

I have been awarded the [MITACS](https://www.mitacs.ca/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"} Globalink Research Award twice. The first internship took me to [INRS](https://inrs.ca/en/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"} in Montréal, where I worked with [Prof. Leszek Szczecinski](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=elDOrQIAAAAJ&hl=en){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"} on parameter-efficient fine-tuning for vision-language models (CLIP-LoRA-XS). The second brought me to [Université Laval](https://www.ulaval.ca/en){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"} in Québec City, where I work with [Prof. Chengkai Fan](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=qsHbxsMAAAAJ&hl=en){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"} on machine learning for sustainable mining engineering.

My capstone project applied NASA and IBM's Prithvi-EO-2.0 foundation model to detect endangered *araucaria angustifolia* canopy from satellite imagery, supervised by [Prof. Daniel Cavalcanti](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=owEu8FkAAAAJ&hl=en){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"} at UTFPR. I have also published work on IoT platform scalability at WPerformance 2025.
