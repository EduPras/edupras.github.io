**Supervised by [Prof. Edson Tavares Camargo](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=BTv_40QAAAAJ&hl){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"} (UTFPR) · Funded by Araucaria Research Grant · [Published](/publication/2025-07-01-scalability-evaluation-sentilo)**

As a Research Assistant, I created a containerized solution for [Sentilo](https://www.sentilo.io/). The focus of the research was to create a cost-effective alternative solution to monitor the air quality, using low-cost sensors. I added layers to communicate with ElasticSearch, using Logstash to capture the data arriving on Redis. Later, this project was used to test the scalability.
